Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our training programs and support.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2 hours during business hours (9 AM - 8 PM EST, Monday-Friday). For urgent matters, our 24/7 live chat support is available for immediate assistance.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer free 30-minute consultation calls with our CCIE-certified experts. This is perfect for discussing your career goals, choosing the right course, and getting personalized study recommendations.

What support is available after I enroll?

Our students get comprehensive support including: 24/7 access to instructors, weekly office hours, peer study groups, technical lab support, and career guidance. We're committed to your success throughout your entire journey.

Can I speak with current students or graduates?

Absolutely! We can connect you with current students and recent graduates who can share their experiences. We also have regular alumni webinars and a private community where you can network and ask questions.

Do you offer payment plans or financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to make our training accessible. Options include monthly installments, employer sponsorship assistance, and early-bird discounts. Contact us to discuss the best option for your situation.

What makes your training different from other providers?

Our training combines real-world experience with structured learning. All instructors are active CCIE professionals, we provide unlimited lab access, offer a pass guarantee, and maintain small class sizes for personalized attention.
